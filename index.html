IRO — Issues Reporting Outlet

A shared channel for AAUA students and Akungba, Iwaro, Etioro & Ayegunle indigenes to report and resolve community issues together.

Why IRO exists

Town–gown friction between the university community and host indigenes often goes unheard because there is no shared, trusted channel to raise it. IRO gives both sides one place to speak and be tracked.

  • Report an issue in under a minute — no jargon, no complex forms
  • See what's already been reported, so concerns aren't duplicated
  • Track a report from submission to resolution
  • Open to both students and indigenes, by design

How it works

Submit a report → an admin reviews and categorizes it → it's escalated to the right authority → status updates as it moves toward resolution.
